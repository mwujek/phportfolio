Education

Casey Reas began his undergraduate work at the School of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning at the University of Cincinnati. After receiving his bachelors degree, Reas went on to pursue a masters degree at MIT. During this time, Reas learned from the well-known graphic designer and computer scientist, John Maeda. He also collaborated with Ben Fry to develop their research to increase software literacy and promote digital visual arts. Currently, Reas is a professor at UCLA in the Media Arts Department.
