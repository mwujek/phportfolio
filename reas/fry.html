Ben Fry

Ben Fry is a data visualization designer. He is principal of Fathom, a design and software consultancy based in Boston, MA. Fry partnered up with Casey Reas to develop Processing. This partnership has authored several publications including:

  • Getting Started With Processing
  • Processubg: A Programming Handbook for Visual Designers and Artists.
  • Visualizing Data
    •

Like Reas, Fry also attended Massachusetts Institute of Technology and he received his doctorate in Aesthetics & Computation.
