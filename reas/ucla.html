Reas @ UCLA

Casey Reas currently works at UCLA in the Department of Design Media Arts (DMA). His involvement in this program is reflected in the progression of the UCLA Arts Software Studio. This is a group of students and faculty who develop software within the context of the arts by creating, distributing, and critiquing software. The defining characteristics of this community include:

  • - cultivatation of discussion around software and arts.
  • - fund and assist graduate students who work with new media.
  • - provide infrastructure and knowledge around creating software.

Reas expertise in software programming and artistic sensibility have been an invaluable asset to the DMA program.
