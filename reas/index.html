Casey Reas:
Non-linear biography

As our society becomes increasingly intertwined with technology, the once clear boundaries between virtual and reality are becoming less distinct. Although many naturally evolving systems cannot be re-created by a machine, their forms and likeness is often emulated in digital art and design. The computing power and resources that contemporary artists and designers have access to is absolutely astounding. The use of digital tools has inspired collaboration, communicative transparency, and creative sharing that is unparalleled in previous eras. By taking advantage of powerful digital media, the traditional and organic process of creation may be overshadowed by programs that eliminate tedious tasks and automate programable forms.
