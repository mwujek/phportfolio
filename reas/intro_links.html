Casey Reas

Casey Reas is an artist, designer, and programmer currently based in Los Angeles, CA. His formal education began at the School of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning at the University of Cincinnati. He later studied at Massachusetts Institute of Technology and received a graduate degree in Media Arts and Sciences. Reas and Ben Fry, a colleague at MIT, developed a program that combined their interests of computer science, art, graphic design, data mining, and data visualization.
